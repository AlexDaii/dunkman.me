About Andrew (that’s me!)

“Every day, think as you wake up, today I am fortunate to be alive, I have a precious human life, I am not going to waste it. I am going to use all my energies to develop myself, to expand my heart out to others; to achieve enlightenment for the benefit of all beings. I am going to have kind thoughts towards others, I am not going to get angry or think badly about others. I am going to benefit others as much as I can.” ― Dalai Lama XIV

I’m a sofware developer specializing in web and usability, currently working for Harvest. I tend to spend much of my time in JavaScript and CoffeeScript, but have worked with a variety of languages, libraries, and frameworks in the past in and around the web.

Currently a citizen of Washington, D.C. — you can generally find me anywhere reachable by bike or Metro. You can find me around the ‘tubes on Twitter and GitHub, of course.
